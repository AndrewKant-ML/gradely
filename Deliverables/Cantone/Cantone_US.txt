User Stories:

1) As a student,
I want to reserve a seat at exams,
so that i can be sure i'll have a seat
the day i want to take the tests


2) As a teacher,
I want to insert exams dates and
reservations deadlines,
So that my students will be able to
reserve their seats in advance and I'll
be able to choose the right room


3) As a degree course admin,
I want to create pages for all the
classes of the degree course i admin,
So that students can join the courses
and get all the infos about lessons and
exams
